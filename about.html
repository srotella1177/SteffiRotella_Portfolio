About the Artist


Hi There!

My name is Steffi and I am an artist based in Rhode Island. Currently, I am a Studio Arts major at Rhode Island College with a focus in Graphic Design. I work in traditional and digital mediums, and also enjoy creative writing, doing photography and film.

The content of my work admittedly leans more on the dark side. I love richer color palettes and moody tones. Inspirations for my work comes from a variety of places, such as the work of directors Tim Burton and David Lynch, authors like Edgar Allan Poe, and other artists like Van Gogh, Monet and Michelangelo.
